What is ASUS Perfect Warranty ?

ASUS Perfect Warranty provides extra protection for your ASUS device against accidents. With no additional fees, experience peace of mind beyond the standard warranty. It ensures your device is covered against drops, spills, and accidental breakage — because we understand that accidents happen.

Covered from Every Angle

ASUS Perfect Warranty protects your device from life’s unexpected accidents — giving you peace of mind inside and out.
